Patient Engagement Service Focused on Improving Follow-Up Care in Developing Countries

  • Low-Cost Solution for Health Facilities
    Providing a Patient Care Coordinator at a healthcare facility to collect patient information with consent and engaging patients
  • Measure Health Outcomes
    Collecting data to measure patient satisfaction and improve healthcare facility performance
  • Engage Patients For Appointments and Medication
    Contacting patients using phone calls and text messages to engage patients about appointments and medication

Improving patient engagement will solve many issues with adherence to treatment, which is especially important in developing countries when access is limited.
This will ensure that patients with life-threatening conditions such as malaria, TB, and HIV, have the necessary treatment and support.

dawaCare provides the following:

Patient Care Coordinator
Appointment Reminders for Follow-Up
Medication Reminders for Adherence
Improve Patient Management
Data Collection for Measurable Impact
Provide Patient Satisfaction Review for Doctors

Pilot Program

Launched March 2017 in St. Veronica's Polyclinic in Buea, Cameroon

Patient Care Coordinator

Supported over 150 patients, resolved over 50 medication incidents, increased follow-up appointments by 30%

Patient Engagement

Software for the Patient Care Coordinator to scale patient engagement to manage more patients

Process

Developed service while learning workflow and process in the healthcare facility in collaboration with healthcare providers
